description: 'On July 14th, 2025, Cloudflare made a change to our service topologies
  that caused an outage for 1.1.1.1 on the edge, resulting in downtime for 62 minutes
  for customers using the 1.1.1.1 public DNS Resolver as well as intermittent degradation
  of service for Gateway DNS.


  We’re deeply sorry for this outage.


  This outage was the result of an internal configuration error and not the result
  of an attack or a BGP hijack. In this blog post, we’re going to talk about what
  the failure was, why it occurred, and what we’re doing to make sure this doesn’t
  happen again.'

description: I am a software engineer, and I used to enjoy arguing with people for
  technical correctness. Code reviews, design meetings, mailing-list threads, dinner
  tables. If someone was wrong, I wanted them to know it, and I wanted them to know
  exactly why. I collected counterarguments the way I collected patches. I believed
  that if I just laid out the logic clearly enough, the other person would have no
  choice but to come around. Truth would win.

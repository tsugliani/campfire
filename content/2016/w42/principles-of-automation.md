  technology discipline these days. There’s lots of talk about tooling, practices,

  of source code or technical whitepapers. While these are obviously valuable, they
  don’t  usually cover some of the fundamental basics that could prove useful to the
  reader who wishes to perform similar things in their own organization, but may have
  different technical requirements.
